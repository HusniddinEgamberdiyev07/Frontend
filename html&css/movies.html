Spaghetti Carbonara

Cooking time 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta with garlic.
  3. Beat eggs with Parmesan.
  4. Mix pasta with pancetta, remove from heat, stir in egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt & pepper, serve hot.
